About Us

01. Company Overview

Rejuvenate Your Industrial and Aviation Power

Flysquawk Integrated Sdn Bhd is a Malaysian-based Maintenance, Repair, and Overhaul (MRO) partner and asset management solutions provider, specialising in aviation engines, industrial aero-derivative turbines, steam turbines, centrifugal compressors, centrifugal pumps, and non-destructive testing (NDT).

In 2025, Flysquawk was officially recognised as the MRO Partner for PT Nusantara Turbin dan Propulsi (NTP) ‚Äî Indonesia's leading turbine and propulsion specialist ‚Äî specifically for rotating equipment services in Malaysia's palm oil industry.

This appointment covers steam turbines, centrifugal compressors, centrifugal pumps, and reverse engineering of rotating components, supporting mill operators, refineries, and green energy plants throughout Malaysia.

Through this partnership, Flysquawk connects NTP's 36 years of engineering expertise with Malaysia's industrial and agricultural energy sectors ‚Äî delivering global-standard maintenance quality, local agility, and sustainable lifecycle solutions that enhance production reliability and energy efficiency.

02. Vision

To be the leading enabler of sustainable industrial and rotating equipment MRO solutions in Malaysia and ASEAN ‚Äî empowering a decarbonised future through energy-efficient innovations and partnerships.

Mission

  • Represent and promote PT NTP's advanced turbine and rotating equipment capabilities across Malaysia and ASEAN

  • Support clean energy transitions through efficient steam and mechanical power recovery systems
  • Build long-term partnerships with plantation groups, energy operators, and government-linked companies (GLCs)
